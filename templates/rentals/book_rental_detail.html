Rental Terms & Conditions
Rental Period

Books must be returned by the due date. Late returns will incur a fee of ৳{{ settings.daily_late_fee }} per day. You can renew your rental up to {{ settings.max_renewals }} times before the due date to avoid late fees.

Book Condition

Books must be returned in the same condition as rented. Please avoid:

  • Writing or marking inside the book
  • Water damage or stains
  • Bent pages or spine damage
  • Missing pages or cover damage

Damage beyond normal wear and tear will result in additional charges.

Security Deposit

A security deposit of ৳{{ settings.security_deposit_amount }} per user (one-time) is required. This will be:

  • Charged only once for your first rental
  • Fully refunded upon timely return of all books in good condition
  • Adjusted for damage repairs if necessary
  • Refunded within 5-7 business days after return verification
Renewals

You can renew your rental up to {{ settings.max_renewals }} times before the due date at no additional cost (except rental extension fees if applicable). Renewals can be done through your account dashboard.

Cancellation Policy
  • Pending rentals: Can be cancelled anytime before activation with full refund
  • Active rentals: Cannot be cancelled once started
  • Cancelled rentals will have their security deposit refunded within 5-7 business days
Return Process

To return a book:

  1. Visit your My Rentals dashboard
  2. Click Return Book on your active rental
  3. Bring the book to our store or arrange pickup
  4. Our team will verify the condition
  5. Receive confirmation and refund within 5-7 days
